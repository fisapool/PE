ProxyEthica Connection Sharing

What We're Asking

We're asking for your consent to share a small portion of your unused internet bandwidth to help power our residential proxy network. This helps other users access content that might be geographically restricted.

How It Works

When you opt in:

  • We'll only use your connection when your device is idle
  • We'll only activate sharing when you're on WiFi (never mobile data)
  • We'll only use your connection when your device is charging
  • We strictly limit bandwidth usage to 100MB per day maximum
  • You can monitor usage and opt out at any time

What You Get in Return

As a thank you for sharing your connection, you'll receive:

  • Premium features in our application
  • Access to our own proxy network when you need it
  • Support for services that rely on a distributed network

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All traffic through your connection is encrypted
  • We don't store any browsing data on your device
  • We have strict content policies prohibiting illegal activities
  • We use advanced filtering to prevent malicious usage

Your Control

You have complete control:

  • View real-time bandwidth usage in the app
  • Pause sharing anytime through settings
  • Set custom limits on when sharing is allowed
  • Opt out completely whenever you want
